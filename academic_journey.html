My Academic Journey

  • Academic Performance & Educational Qualifications...
  • Initial Education
  • I started my primary education in a local MCD primary school nearby where I live. I am a bright student since childhood because of my elder brother who used to teach me all the stuff in advance.
  • I used to secure positions in class very easily without making that much effort.....thanks to my catching power and intuition.
  • After completion of elementary knowledge (5th class) I changed school to another local Government school which was also nearby.
  • Secondary Education
  • After elementary education I took admission in Government Sarvodaya Co-ed Sr. Secondary School which was about 300 Meter from where I live.
  • During 6th - 8th I mainly focused on learning Stunts, Dance. I used to play cricket, Badminton and other games and hang out all the time to explore new places in Delhi with my friends which helped me Developing sense about how things works in actual.
  • I was quite good at studies and needed not much efforts to secure positions (90% marks) in those days
  • I used to participate in various competitions like Mental Math, Essay Writing, Badminton tournaments and many more...
  • Since the marks of 10th matters so I put all my efforts to achieve higher marks in matriculation and secured 9.6 CGPA.
  • Higher Education ( 11th & 12th)
  • 11th class was full of experience in my life. I learnt a lot during this year a drastic change in syllabus along with classmates bring something special to me.
  • In 12th class I used to study for 5-6 hours a day.
  • It was the first time in my life when I felt the need to make a time table and moderately followed it. And yes, making time table surely enhances your efficiency and productivity to a great extent.
  • Not just time table but also strategy about how to study and which one should be given attention now and what should be left for later. I made plans for every aspect.
  • And finally the "hard work pays off" and I secured 90.25% in my board exams
  • College Life..._Graduation
  • I had a great interest in Economics, there is something in this subject I feel like I am familiar with it.
  • So I decided to pursue major in Economics and started my search and analysis about this course.
  • I took admission in Atma Ram Sanatan Dharma College (University of Delhi) in 2019.
  • In college I get an exposure of students with different background from all over India.
  • I worked on my communication and Personality Development.
